Our Services

Family Ride

Relaxing coastal cruise suitable for families with calm waters, snacks, and plenty of stops for photos and short swims.

Breakfast / Lunch at Sea

Enjoy a freshly prepared meal anchored over clear water. Choose breakfast or lunch — we serve local favorites and light continental dishes.

Diving & Snorkeling Adventure

Visit the famous underwater shipwreck and sunken airplane. Guided snorkeling for beginners and optional guided dives for certified divers.

Why guests love us

Private & Comfortable

Small groups, comfortable seating, shade, and a crew focused on your safety and enjoyment.

Local Knowledge

Our crew knows the best snorkeling spots and the safest anchor points near the wrecks.

English & Arabic Crew

We speak both Arabic and English — we make reservations and tours easy for international visitors.
